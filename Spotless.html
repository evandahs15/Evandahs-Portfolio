About Project

This projects looks at differentiating a cleaning company for executives by creating a more personal user experience with a popping look.

The process

  • 1. User experience
  • 2. Brand Identity
  • 3. Web design
  • 4. App interface design

Behind the
scenes

This project started at the heart of the user. I interviewed four executives to understand how they are currently making decisions.

Next, I conducted user testing on other websites to understand the current user journey.

The user research helped me understand how to improve the experience. This included focusing on a clear heirachy, unique selling point, and clear categorization within the content strategy.

From here, I decided to use a high contrast color pallete to create a strong heirachy, and point of difference throughout the website and application.
