Behind the scenes

Theres always a beggining to an end. This project started at the heart of the user. I interviewed four executives to understand how they are currently making decisions.

Then I conducted user testing on other websites helped map out the current user journey.

The user research led to many decisions about the content strategy. This included focusing on a clear heirachy, unique selling point, and clear categorization.

Based of the user research I decided to use high contract colors to help create a stronger heirachy throughout the website and application.

From here I started building wireframes for prototyping, which lead to the final product before your eyes.
