About

I am passionate about translating innovative product visions into beautiful designs to help reach market.

In 2018, I graduated from Auckland, Unitec with a Bachelor in Creative Enterprises, majoring in UI/UX design and was granted Scholorship to learn Full Stack developement at the all immersive coding bootcamp -Enspiral Dev Academy.

I come from a background in business, in particular corporate cultral change. I am interested in design as a means to empower business to impact people. I am passionate about product developement and innovation.
