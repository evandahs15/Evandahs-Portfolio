About me

Coming from a start-up background my goal is to work with businesses to make thier vision a reality.

I started my career as an event manager for large corporates, then shifted to a start-up owner. I learned very fast how difficult communicating a product vision is with designers and developers. I became a UI / UX designer to help businesses overcome this and bring thier product to life.

In 2018, I graduated from Auckland, Unitec with a Bachelor in Creative Enterprises, majoring in UI / UX design. Then recieved a certificate in Full-stack developement at Auckland, Enspiral Dev Academy. Currently, I am working at Datacom but love to talk to any business owners with a product vision.
